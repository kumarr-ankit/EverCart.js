Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 21, 2025
This Privacy Policy explains how EverCart (“we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses, shares and protects information when you use our website or mobile app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: name, email, phone number, password, address.
  • Transaction data: orders, products, prices, payment method (we do not store full card data).
  • Referral & partner data: referral codes and partner IDs entered by users.
  • Device & usage data: IP address, device type, browser, pages visited, and activity logs.
  • Support and reviews: messages you send to support, product reviews and ratings.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account, process orders, and provide customer support.
  • To process payments and verify transactions (via our payment partners).
  • To personalize your experience and show relevant products, offers and recommendations.
  • To analyze performance and generate analytics for business partners (aggregated and anonymized where possible).
  • To detect and prevent fraud, enforce our terms, and comply with legal obligations.

3. Sharing & Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Payment processors: for payment authorization and settlement.
  • Delivery partners: to fulfill and track orders.
  • Business partners: when a referral code links your account to a partner (they see limited order/performance data related to your purchases under that referral).
  • Service providers: analytics, hosting, email, and customer support providers operating on our behalf.
  • Legal reasons: if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of EverCart or others.

We do not sell your personal data to third-party marketers.

4. Cookies & Similar Technologies

We use cookies, local storage and similar technologies to remember preferences, enable core functionality, and collect analytics. You can control cookies via your browser settings, but disabling them may affect the site’s functionality.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no online system is completely secure — if you suspect unauthorized access to your account, contact support immediately.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account and transactional data as long as needed to provide services and to comply with legal obligations. We may keep anonymized or aggregated data for analytics indefinitely.

7. Children

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have such data, contact us to remove it.

8. Your Rights

  • Access and review the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request corrections or deletion of your personal data (subject to legal limits).
  • Opt-out of certain marketing communications.

To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will post the revised policy with a new “Last updated” date. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
